Mutliple Fires Burn Across Los Angeles

By Josephine White

According to the Los Angeles Times over 130,000 people are being ordered to evacuate the LA area and over 2,000 buildings have been destroyed due to wildfires burning across LA county.

Four widlfires have erupted across Los Angeles throughout the past week, with the two largest blazes: the Palisades fire and the Eaton Fire still burning today.

Driven by winds reaching over 60 miles per hour, the two fires have ingulfed over 37,000 acres combined; more than double the size of Manhattan.

Status of the fires:

  • Palisades Fire: Ongoing; 23,000+ acres burned
  • Eaton Fire: Ongoing; 14,000+ acres burned
  • Kennywood Fire: Extinguished; 1,000+ acres burned
  • Hurst Fire: Extinguished; 500+ acres burned

As of now, eight people have died in the Palisades fire and 17 lives were taken by the Eaton fire, though the deathtoll is expected to climb.
